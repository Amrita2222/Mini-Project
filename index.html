The Role of Technology in
Modern Education

The term "modern education" refers to the contemporary approach to teaching and learning that has evolved in response to societal, technological, and pedagogical advancements.

Its evolving landscape reflects changes in educational philosophies, methodologies, and practices aimed at preparing students for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Modern education marks a departure from traditional teacher-centered, lecture-based instruction towards student-centered, interactive learning experiences.
